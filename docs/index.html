ℹ️ About StealthHumanizer

🥷 What is StealthHumanizer?

A free, open-source AI text humanizer that transforms AI-generated content into natural, human-like writing. It uses advanced multi-pass humanization with 13+ AI providers to produce text that reads as authentically human.

⚡ How It Works

  1. Text Analysis — Analyzes input for AI patterns (perplexity, burstiness, transition words)
  2. Multi-Pass Rewrite — Rewrites with your chosen level, style, and tone
  3. Auto Re-Humanization — Detects remaining AI patterns and rewrites flagged sentences
  4. Score Verification — Loops until your target human score is reached

🏆 Comparison

Compared to StealthWriter ($20-50/mo, 10-150 daily limit) and QuillBot ($19.95/mo, limited features), StealthHumanizer is:

  • ✅ 100% free — just use a free API key (Gemini, Groq, ZAI)
  • ✅ Unlimited usage — no daily caps, no word limits
  • ✅ Open source — MIT License, fully transparent
  • ✅ 13+ AI providers — switch anytime
  • ✅ Private — keys never leave your browser
  • ✅ 13 tone presets — more than most alternatives
  • ✅ Ninja mode — 5-pass auto-humanization targeting 100%
